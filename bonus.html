Nutmegs

Sandy brown

  1. The aim is to kick, roll, dribble, throw, or push the ball (or puck) between an opponent's legs (feet).
  2. Owing to its effectiveness and being visually impressive, it is very popular among players and can be frequently seen being attempted multiple times throughout a game
  3. The use of the word nutmeg to mean leg, in Cockney rhyming slang, has also been put forward as an explanation.
